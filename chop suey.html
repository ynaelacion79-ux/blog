Chop Suey

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, cabbage, bell peppers, snow peas)
  • 200g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tsp cornstarch (dissolved in water)

Steps

  1. Saut√© garlic and onion. Add chicken and cook until white.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 mins.
  3. Pour chicken broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
  4. Simmer for 5 mins.
  5. Add cornstarch slurry to thicken sauce. Serve hot!
