Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate meat in soy sauce, garlic, and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Sear meat in a pot until browned.
  3. Pour marinade and water. Add bay leaves.
  4. Simmer for 30–40 mins until tender.
  5. Add vinegar and simmer 10 mins more (don’t stir initially).
  6. Season with salt and sugar. Serve hot with rice!
