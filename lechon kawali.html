Lechon Kawali

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pork belly
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp peppercorns
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • Cooking oil for deep-frying

Steps

  1. Boil pork belly with garlic, salt, and pepper until tender.
  2. Drain and let cool. Pat dry.
  3. Deep fry pork belly until golden and crispy.
  4. Drain excess oil. Chop into pieces.
  5. Serve with lechon sauce or vinegar dip.
