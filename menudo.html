Menudo

Ingredients

  • 500g pork, cubed
  • 100g liver, cubed
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup water

Steps

  1. Saut√© garlic and onion. Add pork and cook until browned.
  2. Add tomato sauce and water. Simmer for 20 mins.
  3. Add potatoes, carrots, and liver.
  4. Cook until vegetables are soft and sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with rice.
