Halo-Halo

Ingredients

  • Shaved ice
  • Sweetened banana, sweetened beans, jackfruit, nata de coco
  • Leche flan slices
  • Ube halaya
  • Evaporated milk
  • Scoop of ice cream (optional)

Steps

  1. In a tall glass, layer sweetened fruits and beans at the bottom.
  2. Add shaved ice on top until glass is full.
  3. Drizzle evaporated milk generously.
  4. Top with leche flan, ube, and ice cream. Serve cold!
