Kare-Kare

Ingredients

  • 1 kg oxtail or beef shank
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 bunch pechay or bok choy
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 banana heart, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp annatto seeds (for color)
  • Bagoong (shrimp paste) for serving

Steps

  1. Boil oxtail until tender (1.5–2 hrs).
  2. Sauté garlic and onion. Add boiled meat.
  3. Add peanut butter dissolved in some broth.
  4. Add annatto water for color.
  5. Add vegetables and simmer until soft.
  6. Serve hot with bagoong on the side.
