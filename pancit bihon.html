Pancit Bihon

Ingredients

  • 200g bihon noodles
  • 200g chicken breast, shredded
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth

Steps

  1. Saut√© garlic and onion. Add chicken and cook.
  2. Add vegetables and stir-fry 3 mins.
  3. Pour broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
  4. Add bihon noodles and simmer until soft.
  5. Mix well until broth is absorbed. Serve hot.
